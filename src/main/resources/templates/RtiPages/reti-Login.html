High Court
For The State of Telangana

Online Application Portal
(Under the Right to Information Act, 2005)

This online application portal is meant for filing RTI applications
and RTI Appeals in the High Court for the State of Telangana.
  • The fee for the RTI Application is Rs.10/- which has to be paid online through the various options provided in the
    payment module.
  • Please note that Appeals against orders passed on applications submitted physically cannot be filed through
    this online portal.
  • Suggestions for improving this online portal are most welcome
